The restaurant

Our restaurant is a tribute to the frenzy that animated André“Dédé” Fortin , charismatic singer of the popular group “Les Colocs” from Normandin in Lac-Saint-Jean

Just like Dédé Fortin, our lunches and dinners are filled with a contagious frenzy! Whether early in the morning or in the middle of the day,our dishes are appetizing and always prepared with passion and appreciated by our customers.


Comfortably seated, you can fully appreciate the freshness of our fresh fruits, our wide selection of dishes and our delicious daily menus.

With family or friends, you will find yourself in a warm and urban setting embellished with the memories and lyrics of this renowned singer-songwriter, founding member of the group Les Colocs.





It is therefore not surprising to note that Dédélicieux's passion for the freshness and taste of food makes it one of the best restaurants in Saguenay–Lac-Saint-Jean.
